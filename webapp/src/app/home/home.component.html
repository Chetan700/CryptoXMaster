KuCoin -- End of Life Notice

I have decided to end of life the KuCoin scanner. Less than 1% of the connections to this site are for KuCoin, and due to some initial design choices, it makes it harder to build enhancements into the Binance scanner.

It may come back in the future when I've built in better support for a multi-exchange scanner. But for now this scanner will remain, I'll restart it when it gets glitchy but no further work will be done on it.

I have a left a copy of the KuCoin scanner running at an alternate URL. I will restart it as needed but do not plan to maintain it.

https://kucoin.cryptoxscanner.com
